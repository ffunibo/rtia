Lavori a Roskilde, DK

The end of the session at the Russell Tribunal in Roskilde, Denmark, which was to judge war crimes committed by American in Vietnam, on December 4, 1967. In the middle, from left to right: the French writer Simone DE BEAUVOIR, the philosopher, writer and dramatic author Jean-Paul SARTRE, the Yugoslav historian Vladimir DEDIJER, the mathematician and the Tribunal's President Laurent SCHWARTZ, who is reading the resolution. At the close of this session, the United States was delcared guilty of many war crimes.
